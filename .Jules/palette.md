## 2026-04-07 - Accessibility Foundations
**Learning:** For static/hybrid landing pages, a "Skip to Content" link and visible focus indicators are essential but often overlooked. Manual CSS for .sr-only and .skip-link:focus is more reliable in this context than relying solely on utility classes.
**Action:** Always check for Skip links and focus indicators in initial exploration. Use aria-pressed for custom toggle-like buttons (e.g., service selection).
