## 2025-05-15 - Enhancing Form Grouping and Keyboard Navigation
**Learning:** Using semantic `<fieldset>` and `<legend>` for related form controls (like service selection buttons) significantly improves the experience for screen reader users by providing context that a simple `<label>` or `<div>` might miss. Additionally, a "Skip to Content" link is a critical first-step for keyboard accessibility in sites with complex navigation.
**Action:** Always wrap logical groups of interactive elements in a fieldset/legend and ensure skip links are present and styled for high visibility on focus.
